Mastering Liar's Poker via Self-Play and Reinforcement Learning

Researchers have created Solly, the first AI agent to achieve elite human play in reduced-format Liar's Poker, a game with extensive multi-player engagement. Solly uses self-play and a deep reinforcement learning algorithm to outperform large language models and world-class human players, winning over 50% of hands and adapting to various bidding strategies. This achievement showcases the power of self-play and reinforcement learning in mastering complex games and decision-making processes.
